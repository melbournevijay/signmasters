Mesh Banner

Mesh banners are made from a PVC mesh banner material gauze. The printed mesh surface contains thousands of tiny holes per square metre, making them effective at reducing wind loading. The wind passes through the holes reducing the sail effect of the banner. Mesh banners, also known as windproof banners, are less likely to be destroyed by high winds and bad weather conditions.
